Kayden Doyle

Indie‑Rock Artist from New York

Kayden Doyle is a dynamic and versatile musician hailing from Long Island, NY — He began drumming in 3rd grade and is now releasing his own music, blending rock, pop, and folk with his introspective lyrics. Kayden has collaborated with bands like Cue The Pilot and Chio, and performed at venues such as The Cutting Room, and Gramercy Theater in NYC.
